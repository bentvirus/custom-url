    • How to make milo

    Make a basic iced Milo. Add 3 to 5 tablespoons of Milo powder, 3 tablespoons of powdered milk, and 1 teaspoon of sugar to a glass.
    Fill the glass about halfway full with hot water, and stir until Milo is fully dissolved.
    Add ice to fill the glass, stir, and enjoy your refreshing ice cold Milo!
